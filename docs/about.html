Brown Forecasting Club

About Us

We are very interested in predicting what will happen before it happens. As a club, we believe that it's very useful — and fun — to know the future. We can actually do this! By doing lots of practice, we become better forecasters, able to predict the world more accurately. We can use this knowledge to take actions that change the world for the better (for example: predicting a pandemic before it happens).

FAQ

What is forecasting?

Forecasting is the skill of predicting the future. We believe that it's a skill that people can get better at with practice. We assign probabilities to events and then score ourselves based on how well our probabilities fit what actually happened (using some scoring rule).

Are you forecasting the weather?

While some of our questions might be about the weather, we're interested in predicting the future more broadly. We want to be able to put accurate probabilities on lots of classes of events.

What types of questions do you forecast?
