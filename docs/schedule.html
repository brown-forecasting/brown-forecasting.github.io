Sat, March 15 — First Annual Tournament

This March, we will host our inaugural intercollegiate forecasting tournament, bringing together top college students from across New England and the Mid-Atlantic to compete for cash prizes. Hosted on Brown’s campus through the Metaculus platform, the event will feature a fast-paced 30-minute speed round followed by a rigorous three-hour forecasting challenge. Participants will sharpen their forecasting abilities through mathematical modeling, base-rate development, and intuitive reasoning. Compete for a chance at a $1,000+ prize pool!
