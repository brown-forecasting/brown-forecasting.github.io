FAQ

How does the tournament work?

During the tournament, teams will make predictions on a variety of real-world events by giving probabilities. Then a few months later, we resolve the questions and the teams that predicted events most accurately will win prizes!

Do I need prior experience?

No! Anyone is welcome, even if they have not forecasted before. All you need is the ability to think critically about the future and make predictions.
